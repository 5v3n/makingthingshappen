Ruby on Rails Development From Hamburg

Sven Kräuter is making things happen. The gained experience of working in both the enterprise and the startup context made him embrace the values of clean code, extreme programming and end-to-end test driven development. Among the languages and frameworks available for developing web apps the one he admires the most is Ruby on Rails. Working with Rails end-to-end the setup of choice includes HAML & SASS for effective HTML5 and CSS3 views enriched with jQuery and unobstrusive JavaScript. Sven prefers developing test driven for example with Rspec/Capybara and Jasmine since it's a great way to ensure quality and spread knowledge.

Although Sven would not declare himself a DevOps he has some knowledge in hosting web apps for example on AWS or heroku. When it comes to the question whether to host on dedicated servers or using cloud services Sven has solutions handy ranging from low budget vision bootstrapping to larger scaled applications. SCM of choice is GIT. Over half a decade experience in software architecture ensure a deep understanding of Rails conventions like the MVC pattern, database optimization and the principles of clean software design in general.
