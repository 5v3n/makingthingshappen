Agile Coaching From Hamburg

Sven Kräuter is making things happen. Half a decade of online projects and some years of experience working with startups made him choose the process tools that had proven themselves as the most effective. No matter if you are a small team of founders that prototype their vision or an established company refining the existing product: there is no room for wasting time.

The weapons of choice to optimize any product development include the principles of lean startup thinking, agile product development like SCRUM and workflow techniques such as kanban. The true value of these tools of the trade emerge from a certain mindset. The primary goal of any of Sven's coaching assignments is to let these values take root to result in a sustainable added value for the venture.
