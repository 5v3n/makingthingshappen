Internet Of Things Workshops And Products From Hamburg

Sven Kräuter is making things happen in cooperation with Julia Kümmel. Part of the maker scene we combine agile & lean virtues with hardware hacking and online communication know how to prototype our vision of future online interaction: physical goods interacting with the internet on your behalf, integrated seamless in you everyday life. The much quoted internet of things.

Workshops include topics for beginners such as "Introduction to Arduino" that can easily be combined with more advanced topics like "Making your Arduino talk with the Internet" or "Wearable Computing". Hardware product development is done in lean startup manner. The time between sketching your idea to the first working physical prototypes, aka the feedback loop is kept short so you can evaluate your product in order to weave in the feedback into the next iteration.
